First Draft GIS doesn't always get it right. You can fix the location for this place in one of two ways.

Your first option is to type in a new latitude and longitude in the details window. In that window, click on the to the right of the latitude and longitude.

Your second option is to select a place using the map. We'll show you some circles at the locations that we think could be the right answer. You'll also be able to click anywhere to set the new location.
